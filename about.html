About Us

NodeX ihub is an organisation that cultivates and accelerates full-stack IoT and embedded systems talents in Africa and provides technology companies with access to a pool of high performing embedded software and hardware engineers.

OUR STORY

Our story started in 2020 as a technology Research & Development Lab in Effurun, Delta State, Nigeria. Our focus was to create smart IoT hardware systems from scratch to perform predictive and maintenance analysis. Due to the shortage of talents in this space, finding the right skill to join us was difficult. In the spring of 2021 we, started a technology-based institute called NodeX ihub, to cultivate and accelerate IoT and hardware talent in Africa.

NodeX ihub has grown to become one of Nigeriaâ€™s IoT and embedded systems tech hubs. Today we continue to grow the IoT and embedded community here in Nigeria, through practical internship programs that cultivate and accelerate talents and also provides them access to paying jobs. Our internship program has trained about 30 developers in Embedded software development, PCB design and IoT.

MISSION

Our mission is to cultivate and accelerate IoT and hardware talent in Africa.

VISION

Our vision is to be a global leading IoT and hardware talent provider and also support rapid innovation for cutting edge technology.
